# Photo Club Hub — a permanent exhibition for your club's photography

Photographers join clubs to showcase their work and get inspiration from the work of their peers.
Inside a club, with its regular face-to-face meetups, this is a time-tested recipe.
But outside a club's border visibility and interaction is lacking. We rely on
- old-school  club websites (with almost no visitors),
- hectic social media (constant distractions), and
- expositons with framed prints (mainly for nearby clubs, friends and family).
**Photo Club Hub** fills the gap: a calm, gallery-like online space dedicated to club photography.

The free iPhone/iPad app presents curated portfolios of club members from many clubs
in one consistent, distraction-free interface.
Visitors browse interactive maps to discover clubs (and photo museums),
search photographers by name or expertise — "portrait", "macro", "landscape" — across club boundaries,
and view each member's selected images as a permanent online exposition.
A companion app generates equivalent web pages for existing club sites,
so the same functionality also reaches Android and Windows/Mac users.

The design principle: **clubs stay in control**.
There is no central server, no account, no fees.
Your member list and photos remain on your own club website; the app merely links to them.
No copies are made, no rights are claimed — unlike social media platforms.
No sensitive personal data is involved, and nearly every field is optional.
Both apps are free and open source (MIT license),
developed in cooperation with Fotobond Brabant-Oost.

Joining is stepwise, at your own pace:

- **Level 1** (name and location — we can enter this for you) puts your club on the map.
- **Level 2** adds your member list via one small file.
- **Level 3** links member portfolios, which can be generated straight from Lightroom.

No software expertise required — just a club member willing to maintain one file,
an effort comparable to routine website upkeep.

*Your club's best work deserves an audience. Put it on the map.*
